One Rep Max

One Rep Max helps you track your weightlifting progress.

Cool. How do I get started?

To get started, you'll need a place to store your data. For that, we turn to MongoHQ's hosted (and free) database offering:

  1. Sign up for a free account at mongohq.com.
  2. Create a free ("sandbox") database to hold your data. Name it anything you like. We recommend <your-mongohq-username>-one-rep-max.
  3. Find your API key on the "My Account" page on mongohq.com.

Now you're ready to rock. Enter your API key and database name below, and start lifting.

Wait. Is this thing secure?

Well, it depends. Here's what you need to know:

  1. No data is ever sent to the One Rep Max servers. Your browser has downloaded the entire app, and from here on out, the app only sends data to MongoHQ.
  2. All communication between your browser and mongohq.com is encrypted via HTTPS.
  3. For convenience, One Rep Max stores your API key and database name in a cookie. If a nefarious soul gets access to your browser, he could obtain your API key, giving him full access to all your MongoHQ databases. Secure your device accordingly.
